Our Story

The Pitch is Toronto's next big soccer platform — a space where youth in Scarborough can discover their potential on and off the pitch. No barriers, no red tape. Just the game we all love.

Mission

Elevating the standard of soccer in Toronto through excellence, innovation, and community engagement — starting in Scarborough.

Vision

To become Toronto's leading soccer organization — the go-to place to play the beautiful game in the GTA.

From The Founder
Raghav Monga
Founder, The Pitch

Soccer should
be for everyone.

Growing up in Scarborough, I saw firsthand how powerful the game can be. It brings people together across cultures, backgrounds, and experiences in a way few things can. At its core, soccer is the world's game because it asks for so little. All you need is a ball and a place to play. But despite that simplicity, access to structured, high-quality opportunities isn't always there for everyone. That's the gap The Pitch was created to fill.

The Pitch is built by players, for players. It's designed with a real understanding of what the game means to the people who play it and what's often missing in traditional systems. This is more than just a league. It's a space where players can develop their skills, compete at a high level, and be part of something consistent and meaningful within their community.

Our goal is to grow the love of the game across Scarborough and throughout Toronto, while building something bigger than just a season or a tournament. We're working toward becoming the go-to place to play soccer in the city.

At the end of the day, this is about community. It's about giving people a place to belong, to compete, and to grow through the game we all love.

See you on the pitch!

— Raghav Monga

About the Program

Free. Structured.
For Everyone.

The Pitch Summer 2026 Clinic is a free 6-week soccer program for youth aged 18 and under in Scarborough. Every Wednesday afternoon, we bring together young athletes for professional coaching, skill development, real competition, and community.

Playing sports changes lives. It builds confidence, teaches teamwork, keeps you active, and gives you something to look forward to every week. We believe every kid deserves access to that — regardless of their background or what they can afford.

This isn't just a clinic. It's a place to belong. Show up, play hard, and be part of something special.
